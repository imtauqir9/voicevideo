04Step four: Claude writes

Document appears in seconds.

The rise of AI-generated video content in 2025

In the space of eighteen months, AI video has gone from awkward novelty to production-ready tool. The shift is most visible in three places: marketing teams replacing agency reels with prompt-driven shots, educators generating multilingual lessons in minutes, and indie creators producing weekly series that would have demanded a film crew.

But the speed has outrun the verification. Models routinely fabricate statistics, attribute quotes to the wrong person, and confidently describe products that do not exist. The first wave of tools focused on generation. The second wave is starting to focus on something more important: trust.

This piece looks at where AI video creation is today, why the verification gap matters more than the model improvements, and what...
