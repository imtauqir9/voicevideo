What used to take 6 apps and 3 hours now takes one browser tab and 15 minutes.

🎙️

5-layer AI verification
Before you publish, Claude runs five sequential quality checks. No other content tool does this.

  • Layer 3 — Completeness: checks whether the topic is fully covered
  • Layer 4 — Hallucination scan: catches fabricated names, dates, and statistics
  • Layer 5 — Quality score: rates readability, engagement, and tone match

    5-Layer Verification

    Publish with confidence.

    No other content tool runs your writing through a systematic AI quality review before you hit publish. VoiceVideo does — automatically, on every piece of content.

    1
    Accuracy check Factual
    Flags claims that may be incorrect, outdated, or unverifiable. Gives you specific sentences to review.
    2
    Bias detection Balance
    Identifies one-sided framing, loaded language, and missing perspectives. Scores overall balance 0-100.
    3
    Completeness Coverage
    Checks whether the topic is fully addressed. Lists specific aspects that are missing or underdeveloped.
    4
    Hallucination scan Integrity
    Scans for fabricated names, dates, statistics, or quotes that Claude may have invented confidently.
    5
    Quality score Readability
    Rates engagement level, tone match for your audience, and overall readability. Suggests specific improvements.

    FAQ

    Got questions?

    Everything else is in the docs. Or email us — we reply within 24 hours.

    Do I need to install anything? +
    No. VoiceVideo is a single HTML file that runs in Chrome. Download it, open it, and you're working. There is no Node.js, no Python, no Docker, no app store. Just a browser tab.
    What API keys do I need? +
    On the free plan: a Claude API key (console.anthropic.com — $5 free credit) and an ElevenLabs key (elevenlabs.io — 10,000 chars/month free). On Pro, we manage both for you — you just use the tool.
    How much does it actually cost to run? +
    A full run — write a 700-word essay, voice it, render a 5-minute video — costs approximately $0.04 to $0.08 in API fees. The built-in cost dashboard shows you exactly what every call costs in real time.
    Is my content private? +
    Your content goes directly from your browser to Claude's API and ElevenLabs' API — encrypted, never through our servers. Your API keys stay in your browser's memory and are never stored anywhere.
    Can I use my own ElevenLabs voice clone? +
    Yes — that's the point. Add your Voice ID in the voice selector and every piece of content will be narrated in your own voice. One ID is already pre-configured. You can change it any time.
    What is the 5-layer verification? +
    Before you publish, Claude runs five sequential checks: accuracy, bias/balance, completeness, hallucination scan, and quality score. Each layer gives a specific score and findings. You can run it manually or set it to auto-run on every generation.
    Can I refund if it doesn't work for me? +
    Absolutely. 14-day money-back guarantee on Pro and Team plans. Email us, no explanation needed, full refund processed within 24 hours.
