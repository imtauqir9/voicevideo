Why it's different

Two AI providers have to agree
before you publish.

Most AI tools generate confidently and verify nothing. VoiceVideo runs every document through a five-layer audit, then optionally through a second independent provider — GPT-4, Gemini, or Mistral. Agreement strengthens the trust score. Disagreement gets flagged for human review.
